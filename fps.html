Video Game Taxonomy: FPS

FPS games have a special place in my heart for being the first kind of game I've ever played. Although the story is generally bland, the gameplay is fun.

Overwatch
Overwatch is an FPS with many different characters, all with different abilities and play style. It features some standard FPS gamemodes such as point control and guarding an objective.
Call Of Duty
The Call Of Duty franchise is your generic FPS. You run around and shoot stuff. It's not a favorite of mine, but I couldn't really think of any other FPS off the top of my head.
