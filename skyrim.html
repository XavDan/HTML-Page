Video Game Taxonomy: Skyrim

Skyrim is considered one of the best RPGs of all time. The content is interesting, the lore is extensive, the gameplay is exciting, everything about it screams "glorious". In Skyrim, you get to pick from a select group of diverse races and backrounds, while having the soul of a dragon. This soul allows you to consume dragon's souls and absorb their power. The game allows you to go down the path of a warrior, rogue, wizard, or a combination of the 3. It also features many different proffessions you can use to make gear and money.
